Branding

Mira On The Wall Brand Concept

Mira On the Wall was a design solution to the narrow markets of home decor brands and companies. Most home decor brands cater the designs of their goods to the bohemian artisanal aesthetic. I wanted to design a logo animation, upholstery fabric swatches, silverware designs, dishware designs, and tile flooring designs. I used a 3D rendering program to create digital versions of my designs and then compiled my renderings and logo animation into a retail website.


South Walton Utility Logo Redesign

The South Walton Utility logo redesign was one of the first jobs I took on as a freelance designer. The company is a water resource partner on the Gulf Coast that focuses on water treatment and conservation. In the previous version of their logo, there was a waterdrop and blue, green, and purple waves representing the drinking water, golf courses, and sewer treatment plants. For the new logo, I kept the water drop and the colors to keep the familiarity of the company.
