68th Annual Youth Art Focus

During my internship, I also worked with the Pensacola Museum of Art. The PMA holds an annual art show to introduce the K-12 art students in Escambia county to the Pensacola art community. I was incharge of the title wall design and installation, the postcard design, as well as the award certificate and button designs.

BFA Exit Show - Spring 2022

In my internship, I also designed the exhibition identity for the Spring 2022 BFA Exit Show. The main characteristic of the identity is a three tone gradient. The show is exhibited in two locations: half in the UWF gallery and half in the PMA gallery. The title walls for both locations only used 2 gradient tones so that it would be a continuation across locations. I allude to this in the information details on the poster.

BFA Exit Show Postcard - Fall 2021

The Fall BFA Exit Show at the University of West Florida was the second exhbition identity that I designed during my internship with the campus art gallery. The title wall was a motion graphic of the exhibition name 'Synthesis' and I echoed the vertical movement of the letters with gradient stripes in the postcard.

In Place: Faculty Exhibition

In Place was the first exhibition held at the University of West Florida after the reopening post-coronavirus. In my internship with the gallery, I was selected to create the exhibition title and design. I titled the show 'In Place' to spark conversation of what 'working in place' meant to artists during covid.

Coastal Art Museum of St. Augustine

CAMSA is a fictional museum I designed a brand identity for. To showcase the branding, I also designed an opening exhibition concept for the CAMSA gallery. To show how the branding would be used inside the museum, I designed wayfinding signage and gift shop merchandise.
