Unplug Yourself Campaign

With the prevalence of social media rapidly increasing I was asked to design a poster series for social media addiction awareness. There were five different deliverables for the project that included designs for posters, Instagram posts, Twitter banners, Facebook headers, and an infographic. There is a robotic, science-fiction theme throughout my designs to depict the strong ties to social media addiction. The bright colors and flashy textures help bring awareness to the topic by using the same tactics of social media.


UWF Jazz Ensemble Poster Series

In working with the Dr. Grier Williams School of Music, I was tasked with designing a series of posters for UWF Jazz Ensemble and Combo group performances in the 2021-2022 academic year. The general design of the posters stayed consistent throughout the series while changes in the colors and instruments helped differentiate the posters. I used Adobe Illustrator and its global color tool to simplify these color changes.
