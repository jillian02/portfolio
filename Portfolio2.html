Jazz Ensemble Posters

In working with the Dr. Grier Williams School of Music., I was also in charge of designing a series of posters advertising the UWF Jazz Ensemble and Combo group performances for the 2021-2022 academic year. The series consisted of five different posters for five different performances. The general design of the posters stayed consistent throughout the series while changes in the colors and instruments helped differentiate the posters. I used Adobe Illustrator and its global color tool to simplify these color changes.
