The Unplug Yourself Campaign

Over the past few years, there has been a noticeable increase in technology dependence. More specifically, an increase in addiction to social media. Unplug yourself was a multi-platform campaign to spread awareness of these new-age addictions. I designed posters, as well as social media posts for platforms like Instagram, Facebook, and Twitter.

Jazz Ensemble Concert Posters

While I was a design intern for the University of West Florida, I worked with multiple performing art departments. I was chosen to design the concert posters for UWF Jazz Ensemble for the 2022 academic year.

The MIRA Home Accessories Brand

Mira is a conceptual brand that I created based on the idea of a homegoods supplier who catered toward the gothic fairytale style of interior design. I designed the brand identity, a retail website, and I used 3D software to render possible products to sell on their site.

Sheila Dunn Faculty Recital

While I was a design intern for the University of West Florida, I worked with multiple performing art departments. I was selected to design the poster for the Dr. Grier Williams School of Music Faculty Recital of 2022.

TAG Spring 2022 Exhibition Schedule

Also during my internship for the University of West Florida, I worked closely with the University's art gallery. I was selected to design a calendar for the art gallery exhibition schedule for both Fall 2021 and Spring 2022.
